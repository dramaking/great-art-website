Privacy

Privacy is important. We respect yours. We don't share your personal information with third parties.

Your personal information

You don't need to provide any personal information to browse Great Art website.

If you provide your personal information voluntarily, we will not use, share or disclose personal information collected with third parties except for the stated purpose.

Newsletter

We don’t share information gathered from the email newsletter with any third parties. You may choose to voluntarily provide personal information through this website by subscribing to our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time and your details will be deleted from the database.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to track your usage to analyse overall visitor activities. This helps us to identify ways to improve Great Art’s online content for everyone. Things we track include the navigation pathways, the number of repeat visitors and the length of time spent on the website.

We respect Do not track (DNT) settings in browsers. If you have DNT enabled, we will not set cookies.

We don't share information about individual visitor activities with any third parties.

Changes to this policy

Great Art may periodically update this policy. We’ll notify you about the significant changes to it if you have subscribed to our newsletter.
